About Smart Spend

What is Smart Spend?

SmartSpend is a financial management tool that helps you track your expenses and manage your budget using the 50/30/20 rule.

What is the 50/30/20 rule?

The 50/30/20 rule is a simple budgeting rule that divides your after-tax income into three categories: 50% for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings.

How does Smart Spend work?

SmartSpend allows you to input your financial transactions and categorize them as needs, wants, or savings. It then generates a chart that shows your spending in each category and helps you stay within the 50/30/20 rule. It will then give a recommendation on how to adjust your spending to meet the 50/30/20 rule.

Meet The Team
